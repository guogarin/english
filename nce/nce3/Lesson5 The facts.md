# 1. `Editors of newspapers and magazines often go to extremes to provide their readers with unimportant facts and statistics.`
## 1.1 go to extremes to do sth. 
### 1.1.1 释义
过分地……，在某方面走极端
### 1.1.2 造句：女孩子常常在减肥方面走极端。
> Girls often go to extremes to lose weight.
> 

### 1.1.3 造句：有些父母常常过分地满足孩子的需要。
> Some parents often go to extremes to satisfy the needs of their children.
> 

### 1.1.4 造句：阿尔弗雷德·布洛格斯过分地追求 (seek) 成为白领工人的特权。

>
